PHONE CONNECTION PROCESS


Step 1: Power ON
Turn ON the ESP32.
ESP32 will create its own Wi-Fi hotspot.
Wi-Fi Name: ESP32-DASH
Password: 12345678


Step 2: Connect Phone to Wi-Fi
Go to: Settings -> Wi-Fi
Connect the Wi-Fi


Step 3: Open Browser
Open Chrome or any browser.
Type: 192.168.4.1


Step 4: Dashboard Control
Now you can control:
- Robot Movement
- Pump
- Grass Cutter
- Servo Motors
- Sensor Monitoring
directly from your phone.



NOTE:
- Internet is NOT required. ESP32 creates its own local Wi-Fi network.
- Typical range: 10-30 meters
- Multiple phones can connect at the same time.
